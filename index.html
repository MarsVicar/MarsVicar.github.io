About Us

El Restaurante Ixtapa
162 Exchange Park Ln,
Hillsborough, NC 27278

Open every day but Tuesday.
9:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m.

El Restaurante Ixtapa is located 100 yards due south of Weaver Street Market's main entrance, at the end of downtown Hillsborough's "old bridge," on the south bank of the Eno River.
Your hosts at Ixtapa are Pedro and Nicolasa Muñoz and their adult children, Zuri, Gladys, Eliud and Pedro.
The Muñoz family takes pleasure and pride in doing everything possible to make you happy.
